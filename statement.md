## Problem Statement
Manual scheduling in medical facilities causes inefficiencies and errors.

## Scope of the Project
Console-based system for patients and doctors to manage appointments. Excludes advanced features like payments or mobile app.

## Target Users
Patients, doctors, and clinic admins.

## High-Level Features
- User management (register/login).
- Appointment scheduling (book/view/cancel).
- Reporting (schedules and stats).
